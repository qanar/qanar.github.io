Chainbound tournaments

This event option is for location to run it’s once weekly ChainBound Tournament. ChainBound Tournaments can be either Archon or Sealed. They will always be played in the Solo game variant. ChainBound Tournaments award digital rewards to both the Player and the Archon.

Players will earn 1 AEmberShard for taking part and 1 AEmberShard for each win.

Chains will also be awarded or subtracted from an Archon depending on the final standings. They will be awarded or subtracted in the following way

4-8 Players = 3 Rounds

  • 1 or 0 wins = subtract 1
  • 2 wins = Add 2
  • 3 wins = Add 3

9+ players = 4 rounds

  • 1 or 0 wins = subtract 1
  • 2 wins = No change
  • 3 wins = Add 3
  • 4 wins = Add 4

The power level of the Archon will also be increased if at any point is accumulates the following number of chains

  • 1-6 chains = Power level 1
  • 7-12 chains = Power level 2
  • 13-18 chains = Power level 3
  • 19-24 chains = Power level 4

Once an Archon reaches a power level it cannot go below that level.

Age of Ascension Store Championships

The Format and Variant choices are

  • Archon Solo
  • Archon Reversal
  • Sealed Solo
  • Sealed 2 deck Survival

Details and structures are outlined below

Archon/Sealed Solo and Archon Reversal

16-40 players - 5 rounds of Swiss with a top 8 cut
41-76 players - 6 rounds of Swiss with a top 8 cut

Æmbershards

  • Participation - 5 Æmbershards
  • Per win - 3 Æmbershards
  • Top 8 - 10 Æmbershards
  • Top 4 - 20 Æmbershards
  • 2nd place - 25 Æmbershards
  • Winner - 40 Æmbershards

77 players up - 6 rounds of Swiss with a top 16 cut

Æmbershards

  • Participation - 5 Æmbershards
  • Per win - 3 Æmbershards
  • Top 16 - 5 Æmbershards
  • Top 8 - 10 Æmbershards
  • Top 4 - 20 Æmbershards
  • 2nd place - 25 Æmbershards
  • Winner - 40 Æmbershards

Sealed 2 Deck Survival

Pure Survival with no cut. run until 1 player remains

  • 9-20 players - 8 rounds (max)
  • 21-32 players - 9 rounds (max)
  • 33-64 players - 10 rounds (max)

DO NOT RUN WITH MORE THAN 64 PLAYERS

Æmbershards

  • Participation - 5 Æmbershards
  • Per Game played - 2 Æmbershards
  • 1 win - 2 Æmbershards
  • 2 wins - 5 Æmbershards
  • 3 wins - 8 Æmbershards
  • 4 wins - 8 Æmbershards
  • 5+ wins - 12 Æmbershards
  • Finalist - 25 Æmbershards
  • Winner - 40 Æmbershards

If you do not have enough players then you can run the event as a Casual event. This will allow you to run the event and hand out prizes, but will not award the digital awards such as Æmbershards and Win/Loss record.
